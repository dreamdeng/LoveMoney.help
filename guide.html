  • Taking too long with basic clicking
  • Prioritizing morality over urgency
  • Demonstrates the time pressure dilemma

ü§î The Philosophy Behind the Endings

LoveMoney presents a complex moral dilemma with no perfect solution. Each ending reflects different philosophical approaches to ethics:

  • Deontological Ethics: Some actions are wrong regardless of consequences (High Morality)
  • Utilitarian Ethics: The ends justify the means if they produce the best outcome (Low Morality)
  • Virtue Ethics: Character and intentions matter as much as outcomes (Medium Morality)
  • Tragic Choice: Sometimes there are no good options, only degrees of harm (Failure)
